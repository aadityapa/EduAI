## Full Description

EduAI helps students in Classes 1–10 learn smarter with personalized AI tutoring, interactive quizzes, and progress tracking aligned to CBSE, ICSE, and State boards.

**For Students**
- Watch curriculum-aligned video lessons
- Ask the AI tutor questions anytime
- Take quizzes and earn XP
- Plan study with AI weekly planner

**For Parents**
- Track your child's progress
- Pay school fees securely
- Get attendance and homework alerts

**For Teachers**
- Manage classes and attendance
- Assign homework and quizzes
- View class analytics

Built for Indian schools with Hindi and Marathi support.
