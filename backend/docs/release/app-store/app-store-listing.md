## Description

EduAI is the all-in-one learning platform for Indian students in Classes 1–10, supporting CBSE, ICSE, and State boards.

Students get an AI tutor that explains concepts step-by-step, video lessons, quizzes with instant feedback, and gamified progress tracking.

Parents monitor learning progress, receive school notifications, and pay fees in-app.

Teachers manage attendance, assignments, and class performance from one dashboard.

Available in English, Hindi, and Marathi.
