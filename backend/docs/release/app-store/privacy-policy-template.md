## 1. Introduction

[COMPANY LEGAL NAME] ("EduAI", "we") operates the EduAI platform for schools, students, parents, and teachers. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Account data:** Name, email, phone, school, class, role
- **Learning data:** Course progress, quiz scores, AI tutor conversations
- **Payment data:** Processed by Stripe/Razorpay; we do not store full card numbers
- **Device data:** App version, OS, push tokens
- **Usage analytics:** Feature usage, session duration

## 3. How We Use Information

- Provide educational services and AI tutoring
- Process school fees and subscriptions
- Improve platform safety and content quality
- Send notifications (attendance, homework, billing)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. AI Processing

Student messages to the AI tutor are processed to generate educational responses. Conversations may be logged for safety review. We do not use student conversations to train third-party models without consent.

## 5. Sharing

We share data with:
- **Schools (tenants):** Administrators and teachers within your school
- **Processors:** Cloud hosting (AWS), payment providers, analytics (Sentry)
- **Legal:** When required by law

We do not sell personal information.

## 6. Children's Privacy

EduAI is used by minors under school authorization. Schools act as data controllers; EduAI acts as processor. Parent accounts may access linked child data.

## 7. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, and regular security audits.

## 8. Retention

Account data retained while active + 12 months. AI logs retained 90 days unless longer required by school contract.

Contact privacy@eduai.in for access, correction, or deletion requests (subject to school policies).
